CIO-wowing prompts
Run first

Global disruption watch

Act as an MSC Cargo operations risk analyst. Search public maritime, port, carrier advisory, and trade sources from the last 7 days. Build a disruption brief for MSC-operated cargo movements. Focus on vessel incidents, port congestion, strikes, canal restrictions, sanctions, weather, war-risk zones, and AIS anomalies. Return an executive summary for the CIO, top 10 risks ranked by business impact, what changed in the last 24 hours, and what Scout should keep monitoring every 2 hours.

Proves Scout can watch external business risk across the freight network.
Commercial

Competitive rate movement monitor

Act as an MSC commercial intelligence analyst. Monitor public freight rate, surcharge, GRI, PSS, BAF, CAF, and emergency surcharge updates across MSC, Maersk, CMA CGM, Hapag-Lloyd, COSCO, and ONE for the last 30 days. Focus on Asia-Europe, Transpacific, Mediterranean, Middle East, West Africa, and Central Africa lanes. Return lane, carrier, change type, effective date, customer impact, recommended MSC commercial response, and anything Scout should monitor every 2 hours.

Turns rate-change monitoring into a governed commercial intelligence motion.
Service

Customer advisory digest

Search MSC customer advisories from the last 30 days. Summarize every surcharge, GRI, PSS, schedule change, documentation change, port omission, blank sailing, customs advisory, and emergency notice. Group by trade lane and effective date. Create a customer-service-ready brief with what changed, who is affected, what action customers need to take, and which advisories need proactive outreach today.

Saves customer service and sales from manually reading every advisory update.
Cold chain

Reefer exception intelligence

Act as an MSC iReefer operations analyst. Build a monitoring plan for refrigerated cargo exceptions using iReefer-style telemetry: temperature, humidity, GPS location, door open, off-power events, defrost periods, Controlled Atmosphere, and Cold Treatment compliance. Use public MSC iReefer capabilities and best-practice cold-chain rules. Return the top exception patterns that should trigger alerts for pharma, fruit, frozen food, and flowers.

Makes Scout the operating layer over a real MSC digital product.
Governance

AI consumption control plane

Act as MSC’s AI consumption governance analyst. Build a daily control-plane report for Copilot, Cowork, Scout, Copilot Studio, and agent usage. Show total spend, spend by tool, spend by business unit, top users, inactive licenses, high-consumption anomalies, repeated failed tasks, and estimated business value. Flag anything that risks exceeding the approved monthly cap. Recommend which workloads should run in Cowork, which should run in Scout, and which should not run at all.

Answers cost, governance, and predictability before they block scale.
